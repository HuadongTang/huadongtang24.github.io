About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in, University of Technology Sydney (UTS), supervised by Prof. Qiang Wu. Before that, I obtained my Master's degree (MSc Communication Engineering) at Beijing Jiaotong University July. 2018 and obtained my Bachelor's degree (BEng Electronic and Information Engineering) at Nanjing Agricultural University in Jun. 2016.

My research interests focus on computer vision and deep learning, especially the semantic segmentation and object detection on the autonomous driving scenarios.
